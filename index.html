🔬 The Science

Your ischiocavernosus and bulbocavernosus muscles pump blood into the penis during erection. Stronger = harder, longer-lasting erections.

The PC muscle controls ejaculation. Strengthening it lets you voluntarily delay orgasm — the key to lasting longer.

How to find your pelvic floor muscles

Try to stop urinating midstream — those muscles are your pelvic floor. Use this once to identify them; don't practice during urination regularly.

Alternatively, try pulling your scrotum upward without tensing your abs, glutes, or thighs.

Common mistakes to avoid
  • ❌ Squeezing glutes — isolate pelvic floor only
  • ❌ Holding breath — breathe normally throughout
  • ❌ Tensing abs or thighs — keep them relaxed
  • ❌ Skipping the release — it's as important as the squeeze
  • ❌ Too many reps too fast — quality beats quantity
